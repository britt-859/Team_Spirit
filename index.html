    Background

    Client's name: Christy Serrato

    Company name: PairAnything Inc

    Pair Anything is a global winetech company with a mission to help consumers discover and connect them to local wineries.

    Scope

    The client's main intention is to build a cross-platfrom mobile app similar to the client's current webapp. The app will recommend various combinations of wine and food pairings  to the user and allow them to purchase wines online.   

    Solution

    React Native is our front end design choice based on the cross-platform requirement. We will be using SQLite for the database.
